RisingStars

Upcoming events

The Rising Stars in Materials Science 2025 workshop is on Friday 31st of January 2025. It is hosted by our partners at the Henry Royce Institute, UK. Registration is open until the 6th of January and can be accessed via this page.

This full day event will combine CV workshops, panel discussions and creative networking activities. Priority registration will be given to historically marginalised groups, and funding for travel and/or caring responsibilities are available to aid accessibility. It is suitable for early career researchers - from final year PhD students to those in their first permanent position.
